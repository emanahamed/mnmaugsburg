Where We Build Your Visions MnM Augsburg UG is a newly created export company located in Augsburg, Germany. The company started its operation in September 2000. It exports Heavy machinery; Forklift; Spare parts of different machineries; Water meters; Medical equipments; Industrial Boiler; Dye chemicals and Pharmaceutical raw materials to all over the world. Our Goal Then and Now Is to Provide Quality Products on Time
